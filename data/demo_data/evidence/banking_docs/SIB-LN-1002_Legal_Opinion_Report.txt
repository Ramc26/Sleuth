Dear Sir/Madam,

In response to your request, we have conducted a thorough legal scrutiny of the commercial plot situated in Edappally, Kochi, intended as collateral for Loan ID SIB-LN-1002, applied by Mr. Thomas Kurian. The examination involved a detailed review of the title documents, encumbrance records, and relevant legal registers.

**1. Property Description:**

- **Survey Details:** Plot No. 15, Survey No. 35/2B, Edappally Village, Ernakulam District.
- **Area:** 20 cents.
- **Boundaries:** East - Public Road, West - Private Residential Plot, North - Commercial Building, South - Vacant Land.

**2. Title Verification and Encumbrance:**

Upon reviewing the title deeds, specifically Document No. 2345/2019 registered at the Ernakulam Sub-Registrar Office, it is confirmed that Mr. Thomas Kurian holds a valid title to the property. The title is free from mortgages, charges, leases, or any other encumbrances as per the Encumbrance Certificate (EC) issued for the period from January 1, 2000, to October 10, 2023.

**3. Legal Compliance:**

- **Pattayam:** The property is covered under a valid Pattayam issued by the Land Revenue Department, ensuring the land's classification as commercial.
- **Non-Litigation Certificate:** A Non-Litigation Certificate dated September 30, 2023, from the local Village Office corroborates the absence of any registered litigation affecting the property's title.

**4. Pending Litigation:**

It is pertinent to bring to your attention that there is an active dispute concerning the boundary walls of the said plot. The matter is currently under litigation (OS No. 422/2023) in the Munsiff Court, Ernakulam. The dispute pertains to the demarcation of boundaries shared with the adjoining private residential plot to the West. Although the bank system records indicate a clean status, this ongoing litigation may potentially influence future developments or transactions related to the property.

**Conclusion:**

While the current title verification and encumbrance status suggest a clear title in favor of Mr. Thomas Kurian, the mentioned litigation should be carefully considered in the context of the loan approval process. It is advisable to seek a resolution or settlement of the ongoing dispute to mitigate any potential risk to the bank's interest.

**Recommendation:**

Pending the resolution of the boundary wall dispute, it is recommended to proceed with appropriate caution. The bank may consider obtaining an indemnity bond from Mr. Thomas Kurian to safeguard its interests against any adverse outcomes from the litigation.
